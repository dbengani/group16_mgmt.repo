Eloheh: Rooted in Nature

Founded in 2009, Eloheh began as a community farm in Oregon, rooted in Indigenous wisdom and a deep respect for the Earth.

Its founders—a diverse group of ecologists and educators—set out to create an organization dedicated to sustainable agriculture, cultural restoration, and educational outreach.

Over the years, Eloheh evolved into a leading advocate for regenerative living practices. Today, it offers:

  • Hands-on workshops
  • Eco-literacy curriculums
  • Consulting for sustainable land use

Mission Statement: Healing the land by healing our relationships with it.
