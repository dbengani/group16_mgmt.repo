Eloheh: A Journey Toward Better Healthcare

Founded in 2012, Eloheh began with a vision to bring accessible, meaningful care to underserved and rural communities across the U.S.

What started with natural wellness kits and remote consultations has grown into chronic care programs, digital platforms, and scalable health education.

Major Milestones

  1. 2012: Founded in Indianapolis, IN
  2. 2015: Launched wellness product line
  3. 2018: Introduced telehealth services
  4. 2021: Partnered with 30+ rural clinics
  5. 2024: Rolled out EloCare digital platform

Our Focus Areas

  • Preventive Care Kits
  • Chronic Disease Management
  • Remote Patient Monitoring
  • Health Education Programs
