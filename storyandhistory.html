Our Story

Eloheh was founded in 2012 with a mission to make high-quality healthcare products and services more accessible to underserved communities.

The company began as a small wellness startup focused on natural supplements and telehealth consultations. Within just a few years, Eloheh expanded its offerings to include chronic care management programs, preventative care kits, and remote monitoring solutions for patients managing diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease.

Rooted in compassion, driven by innovation, Eloheh now partners with clinics, nonprofits, and local governments across the U.S. to bridge healthcare gaps with scalable, patient-centered solutions.

Milestones

  • 2012: Eloheh founded in Indianapolis, IN
  • 2015: First direct-to-patient wellness product line launched
  • 2018: Telehealth services introduced
  • 2021: Partnered with 30+ clinics in rural areas
  • 2024: Launched EloCare — AI-powered remote health tracking platform

Mission Statement

To empower healthier lives by delivering innovative, accessible, and compassionate healthcare solutions to all communities.
