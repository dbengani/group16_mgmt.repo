Eloheh: A Journey Toward Better Healthcare

Founded in 2012, Eloheh started as a mission-driven company focused on making healthcare products and services more accessible to those in rural areas, as well as any underserved community.

The company began with natural wellness kits and remote consultations, and has since expanded into chronic care, digital monitoring, and preventive solutions.

Major Milestones

  1. 2012: Founded in Indianapolis, IN
  2. 2015: Launched wellness product line
  3. 2018: Introduced telehealth services
  4. 2021: Partnered with 30+ rural clinics
  5. 2024: Rolled out EloCare platform

Our Focus Areas

  • Preventive Care Kits
  • Chronic Disease Management
  • Remote Patient Monitoring
  • Health Education Programs
