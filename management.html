Meet the Eloheh Leadership Team

Eloheh Health is driven by a leadership team dedicated to making healthcare more human, accessible, and innovative. With a blend of clinical expertise and technological vision, our executives are building a future where quality care is just a heartbeat away.

Executive Team

  • Chief Wellness Officer: Samira Khan - Samira oversees patients to make sure they have a good experience.
